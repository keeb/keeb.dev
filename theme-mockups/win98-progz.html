I made a keylogger

2022-06-12

There is something uniquely educational about building the tools you are taught to fear. When I sat down to write a keylogger, it was not with malicious intent but with the curiosity of someone who wanted to understand how these things actually work under the hood. The Win32 API gives you SetWindowsHookEx, and from there it is a short walk to capturing every keystroke on a system. The experience taught me more about operating system internals, hook chains, and defensive thinking than any textbook ever could. If you want to understand security, sometimes you have to build the thing you are trying to defend against.
