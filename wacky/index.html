The Lore

May 28, 2022 — An Origin Story

From the earliest days I can remember, I have been in love with technology. My first real computer memory was playing Mad Dog McCree, published in 1993 for MS-DOS. I was 8 years old, and I had no idea what I was doing, but I was having fun.

Sucked into this new found hobby, chatting away with anyone and everyone on AOL. Figuring out how ‘progs’ worked consumed me. Decompiling FateX for the first time, hundreds of Visual Basic .frm files cascading across the screen.

Armed with my new handle, and a bit more knowledge, it was time to get back to work. Through AOL I learned to rebuild computers, automate tasks, read and write code. Born and raised on the internet. Thanks, AOL.
